💡 WHY THESE CHANGES ARE SAFE

Local Save (30s):
  • Only affects recovery from app crash
  • Max 30 seconds of data loss (acceptable)
  • Still saves on hide/unload

GPS Watchdog (30s):
  • Still detects failures within 1 minute
  • 30 seconds of running = ~50-100 meters
  • Acceptable trade-off

Garmin Sync (6h):
  • Syncs on login (fresh data immediately)
  • Then 4x daily (still very fresh)
  • Wellness data doesn't change minute-by-minute

Coaching Reminders (daily):
  • Still sends at 8 AM local time
  • Just more efficient scheduling
  • Same user experience

Webhook Retry (30m):
  • Garmin webhooks are queued (not lost)
  • 7-day retention buffer
  • 30-min delay is acceptable
